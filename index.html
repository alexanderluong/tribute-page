Leader of Girls' Generation, Kim Taeyeon

Timeline of Kim Taeyeon's Life:

  • 1989 - Born in Jeonju, North Jeolla, South Korea
  • 2007 - Debuted in girl group, Girls' Generation as leader and main vocal
  • 2015 - Released first solo extended play, I, selling over 115,000 copies
  • 2016 - Released second extended play, Why, topping Billboard's US World Albums Chart
    • 2017 - Released first studio album, My Voice, with title track Fine

"You have to smile. Smile like you're really excited."

- Girls' Generation's Leader, Kim Taeyeon
